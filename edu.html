Brief History: The Government of Maharashtra bestowed upon
the Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan the trust to start an engineering college in 1983.
It resulted in the establishment of Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering,
Shegaon on 13th August 1983 in the rented premises.
